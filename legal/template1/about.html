About Our Firm

A legacy of excellence in legal representation

Our History

Founded in 1999 by Margaret Sterling, Sterling & Associates began as a small practice dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality legal services to the local community. What started with two attorneys and a shared vision has grown into one of the region's most respected law firms.

Over the past 25 years, we have expanded our practice areas, welcomed talented attorneys to our team, and built a reputation for excellence that extends far beyond our original community. Today, we serve clients across the state and have successfully handled cases in federal courts nationwide.

Despite our growth, we remain true to our founding principles: every client deserves dedicated attention, honest counsel, and vigorous representation of their interests.

Our Legal Philosophy

At Sterling & Associates, we believe that effective legal representation requires more than just knowledge of the law—it requires a deep understanding of our clients' goals, concerns, and circumstances.

We take a collaborative approach to legal strategy, working closely with our clients to develop solutions that align with their objectives. Whether negotiating a settlement or litigating in court, we combine thorough preparation with creative problem-solving to achieve results.

We are also committed to preventive law. By helping clients anticipate and avoid legal problems before they arise, we save them time, money, and stress in the long run.
