Criminal Defense

Who We Serve

  • DUI/DWI defense
  • Drug offenses
  • Assault and violent crimes
  • Theft and property crimes
  • White-collar crimes (fraud, embezzlement, tax evasion)
  • Federal criminal defense
  • Appeals and post-conviction relief

Why Choose Sterling & Associates

Our criminal defense team includes former prosecutors who understand how the other side thinks. We investigate every case thoroughly, challenge weak evidence, and present the strongest possible defense. When your freedom is at stake, you need attorneys who will fight for you.
