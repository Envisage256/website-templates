What to Expect

During your initial consultation, you'll meet with one of our experienced attorneys to discuss your legal situation. We'll listen to your concerns, answer your questions, and provide an honest assessment of your options.

Consultation Fee Policy

Initial Consultation: $150 for the first hour

This fee is credited toward your legal fees if you retain our firm.

Free Phone Screening

Not sure if you need an attorney? Call us for a free 15-minute phone screening. We'll help you understand if you need legal representation and which practice area applies to your situation.

Pro Bono Consultations

We offer free consultations for qualifying individuals who cannot afford legal services. Please inquire about our pro bono eligibility requirements.

Your Consultation Includes

  • One-on-one meeting with an experienced attorney
  • Review of relevant documents you bring
  • Assessment of your legal options
  • Explanation of potential strategies
  • Clear fee estimate if you choose to proceed
  • Written summary of our discussion

What to Bring

  • Any documents related to your legal matter
  • A list of questions you want to ask
  • Timeline of relevant events
  • Names and contact information of other parties involved
  • Photo ID
