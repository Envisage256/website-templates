Ave's Tech
Success Story.

PhoneCare was born from a simple observation: Ave needed a professional, honest, and high-tech phone repair solution that neighbors could trust.

Why We Started

For years, phone repairs in Ave were a gamble. Customers often faced low-quality screens, missing screws, and "technicians" who learned on the job using the customer's expensive device.

PhoneCare changed that. We invested in professional micro-soldering equipment, ultrasonic cleaners, and a supply chain of high-grade parts directly from top manufacturers.
