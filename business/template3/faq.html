Phone Care Tips

🔋 Extending Battery Life

Avoid letting your battery drop to 0%. Try to keep it between 20% and 80% for optimal health. Also, avoid extreme heat!

🛡️ Protecting Your Screen

Always use a high-quality tempered glass screen protector and a sturdy case. It’s a small investment that saves big money.

💧 Water Damage First Aid

If your phone gets wet, turn it off immediately! Do NOT charge it. Bring it to us ASAP for professional cleaning.
