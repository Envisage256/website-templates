Frequently Asked Questions

Providing clarity on our work and how you can support.

How is MYORG funded?

We are primarily funded through individual donations, small grants from development partners, and community contributions. We prioritize local resource mobilization to ensure long-term sustainability.

Where does my donation go?

85% of every dollar goes directly to program implementation (scholastic materials, vocational kits, field outreach). 15% is utilized for administrative costs and organizational scaling.

Who are your primary beneficiaries?

Our focus is on the most vulnerable members of Ave Sub County, including orphans and vulnerable children (OVCs), unemployed youth, and women-headed households.

Do you work with other organizations?

Yes, we believe in social cohesion and collaboration. We work closely with the Ave District Local Government, religious institutions, and other NGOs operating in the West Nile region.

Can I visit your project sites?

Absolutely! We welcome donors and partners to visit our office in Ave Sub County and see the impact of our work firsthand. Please contact us in advance to arrange a visit.

How can I apply for support?

Beneficiaries are identified through community-led assessments and parish-level referrals. If you are in Ave Sub County and need support, please visit our office or talk to your local village leader.

Still have questions?

We are here to provide any information you need about our organization and projects.
