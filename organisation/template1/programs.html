Our Programs & Services

Creating sustainable impact through community-led initiatives.

THEMATIC AREA 1

Economic Empowerment & VSLA Support

We empower women and youth through Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLA). These groups provide access to small capital, promote financial literacy, and build a safety net for households.

15+ VSLA Groups

Currently supported across Ave District.

450+ Beneficiaries

Mainly women and youth engaged in micro-business.

THEMATIC AREA 2

Education & Child Protection

We believe every child deserves a safe and quality education. Our interventions include scholastic material distribution, school fees support for orphans, and community-based child protection monitoring.

  • School fees support for 50+ vulnerable children
  • Protection training for 100+ community members
  • Scholastic kits for 500+ pupils annually
THEMATIC AREA 3

SRHR & SGBV Response

Advancing Sexual and Reproductive Health Rights (SRHR) and providing support for survivors of Gender-Based Violence. We foster social cohesion and personal safety through dialogue and response mechanisms.
