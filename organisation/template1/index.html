Who We Are

Sustainable Action for Community Development and Empowerment (MYORG) is a community-led organization based in Ave Sub County. We believe in the power of local solutions to solve local challenges.

Our holistic approach addresses the root causes of poverty, inequality, and social exclusion. By focusing on education, economic empowerment, and protection, we create lasting change that transcends generations.
