Featured Story

From Refugee to Business Leader

"When I arrived in Ave Sub County, I had nothing but my family. Through MYORG's vocational training and VSLA support, I saved enough to start a small grain-milling business. Today, I employ two other youths from the community."

— Abdu M., Program Beneficiary

Sarah's Tailoring Success

Sarah was among the first graduates of our tailoring program. She now runs a successful stall in the local market, providing for her 4 children.

David's Schooling Dream

Through our scholarship fund, David returned to school after 2 years out. He is now the top performing pupil in his parish.

Grace's Healing

After experiencing trauma, Grace received psychosocial support through MYORG. She is now a peer counselor for other young women.
