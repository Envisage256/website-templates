Our Story & Mission

Empowering Ave District since our inception.

Our Journey

MYORG was born out of a collective desire to address the systemic challenges faced by communities in Ave District. Ave Sub County, though rich in spirit, has faced significant hurdles in economic stability, education quality, and social protection.

Initially starting as a small community support group, we have evolved into a fully registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) with a comprehensive strategy for sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower communities through programs that foster economic self-reliance, promote social cohesion, ensure access to quality education, and provide comprehensive protection for all, especially women and children.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our actions.

Inclusion

Ensuring that everyone, regardless of gender or background, has equal opportunity.

Empowerment

Giving community members the tools to lead their own development.

Sustainability

Creating solutions that last and respect our local environment.

Our Progress

2022: Formation

Established by a group of community leaders concerned about youth unemployment and school dropout rates.

2023: Official Registration

Successfully registered as a CBO. Launched the first VSLA group with 30 women members.

2026: Scaling Impact

Expanded to include Child Protection and SRHR programs. Now serving over 10 villages in Ave Sub County.
