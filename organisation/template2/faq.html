Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work, donations, and how you can get involved.

How can I donate to MYORG?

You can donate through our website using the donation form on the 'Donate' page. We accept one-time and monthly contributions. We also accept in-kind donations of scholastic materials and medical supplies.

Where is MYORG located?

Our main office is located in Ave Sub County, Ave District, Aveland. We operate primarily within this region but look to expand our impact.

Can I volunteer internationally?

Yes! We welcome international volunteers who can contribute skills in education, healthcare, and organizational development. Please fill out the volunteer application form on the 'Get Involved' page.

Is MYORG a registered organization?

Yes, MYORG is fully registered as a Community-Based Organization (CBO) with the Ave District Local Government.

How is my donation used?

We are committed to transparency. 85% of all donations go directly to program implementation, while 15% covers administrative and operational costs.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Currently, we pool donations to support schools and groups of vulnerable children to ensure equity. However, we can provide updates on the specific schools or groups your donation supports.
