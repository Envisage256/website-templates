Rooted in Community, Driven by Change

Sustainable Action for Community Development and Empowerment (MYORG) is a registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) dedicated to uplifting the lives of those in Ave Sub County, Ave District, Aveland.

Our Journey

MYORG was founded by a group of passionate community leaders who recognized the urgent need for local solutions to global challenges. Since our inception, we have worked on the ground in Ave District, building trust and delivering tangible results.

We believe that true development must be community-led, sustainable, and inclusive of everyone, regardless of their background.

2021: The Beginning

MYORG established in Ave Sub County with a focus on local welfare.

2022: Growth

Expanded to 3 thematic areas including Education and Economic Empowerment.

2023: Formalization

Official registration as a CBO and launch of the SGBV protection program.

Our Vision

A self-reliant and peaceful community where everyone has access to opportunities for economic growth, education, and safety.

Our Mission

To empower communities through programs that foster economic self-reliance, promote social cohesion, ensure access to quality education, and provide comprehensive protection for all, especially women and children.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We provide the tools and training necessary for individuals to build their own sustainable futures.

Sustainability

Our interventions are designed to be long-lasting and respectful of the local environment and culture.

Community-Led

We listen first. Our projects are always developed in collaboration with local residents.

Inclusion & Equity

We specifically advocate for the rights of women, children, and marginalized groups in Ave District.
