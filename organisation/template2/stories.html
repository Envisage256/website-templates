Evidence of Change

We believe in results. These case studies show how our integrated programs are transforming lives in Ave Sub County.

Before & After

Amina: From Survival to Success

Before MYORG

Unemployed mother of 4. No income. Household income < $1/day. Children out of school.

After 12 Months

Runs tailoring shop. Household income $5/day. All children enrolled in school. Community mentor.

"The training didn't just teach me how to sew; it taught me how to manage a business and my life. MYORG stayed with me until I was stable."

- Amina, Tailoring Graduate

School Transformation

Revitalizing Ave Primary

Condition 2021

Dilapidated blocks, 30% dropout rate, no clean water on site.

Condition 2023

New blocks, 85% attendance, solar lighting, borehole installed.

By providing desks, books, and basic infrastructure, we transformed a failing school into the pride of the Ave Sub County. Enrollment increased by over 200 students in two years.
