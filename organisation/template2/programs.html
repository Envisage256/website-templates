Integrated Community Programs

Our initiatives are designed to address the root causes of poverty and inequality in Ave Sub County and across Ave District.

Welfare & Psychosocial Support

We provide essential counseling and mental health services to survivors of trauma and vulnerable households.

  • Target: 1,500 individuals annually
  • Coverage: All parishes in Ave Sub County
  • Services: Counseling, basic kits, crisis response

Impact Metrics

Since 2021, we have assisted over 400 families with direct welfare support and reached 1,200 people through community counseling sessions.

View Success Stories →

Financial Inclusion

We don't just give resources; we build capacity. Our program combines micro-capital with intensive business management training.

  • Beneficiaries: Women groups and youth entrepreneurs
  • Focus: Agribusiness, retail, and craft production
  • Goal: Average income increase of 50% per participant

Economic Empowerment

Empowering households to achieve financial independence through sustainable livelihoods.

Support a Business

Education & Child Protection

Ensuring safe access to quality learning environments and protecting children from all forms of abuse.

  • Partners: 5 Local Primary Schools
  • Resources: Books, desks, uniforms, solar lamps
  • Protection: Child-friendly safe spaces and monitoring

Geographic Coverage

We currently serve the Ave Sub County area, with plans to expand our protection monitoring system to neighboring sub-counties in Ave District by 2026.

Our child protection committees are active in 12 villages, providing 24/7 emergency response support.

Additional Thematic Focus Areas

Livelihood & Skills

Vocational training in tailoring, carpentry, and sustainable agriculture for youth.

Advocacy

Engagement with local government to ensure community voices are heard in policy making.

SRHR

Sexual and Reproductive Health education and access to services for adolescents.

SGBV

Intensive prevention campaigns and comprehensive support for gender-based violence survivors.
